About

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian businessman and the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries.

Net worth: 11,760 crores USD (2024) Forbes

Born:19 April 1957 (age 67 years), Aden, Yemen

Spouse: Nita Ambani

Children: Anant Ambani, Akash Ambani, Isha Ambani

Reliance Industries Limited is an Indian multinational conglomerate company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was founded by Dhirubhai Ambani in 1958 and is currently led by his son, Mukesh Ambani.In 1981, Ambani was pulled out of Stanford by his father Dhirubhai Ambani to help run their family business.
